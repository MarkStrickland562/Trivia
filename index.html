Who? What? When? Where? Why?

This is based on a well-known child’s game. The game will be played by two teams consisting of one to four players. An image will be displayed along with a question that could be “What is It?” or “Who is It?” or “When Did this Happen?”, etc., along with four possible answers. The team whose turn is up selects the answer they believe is correct. If they guess right, the team score is increased by 1 point. If they’re wrong, the score is decreased by 1 point.
